About Us

We provide models and an interactive tool to find the optimal patient transfers for hospitals that are experiencing a large number of COVID-19 patients. To read more about the problem, please visit the home page.


Team: Felix Parker, Hamilton Sawczuk, Fardin Ganjkhanloo, Farzin Ahmadi, and Professor Kimia Ghobadi

Our team is affiliated with:

The Center for Systems Science and Engineering
The Malone Center for Engineering in Healthcare
The Center for Data Science in Emergency Medicine
Department of Civil and Systems Engineering
Johns Hopkins University
