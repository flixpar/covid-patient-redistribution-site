Hospital Occupancy Dashboard

This interactive dashboard finds the best way to transfer COVID-19 patients between public health units (PHU) in Ontario. You can use it to see which PHU are over capacity, when and how many additional beds are needed, and the optimal patient transfer strategy.

How can we better manage COVID capacity in hospital systems?
With the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, many hospitals are at capacity or are quickly approaching it. Hospitals are coping with the COVID surge through a variety of methods, including opening up new beds, but these are often costly and can hurt the level of care that patients receive. This burden on hospitals can be reduced if we optimally transfer patients from over-capacity hospitals to nearby hospitals that have available space. To find the optimal number of patients to transfer and where to transfer them to, we use mathematical optimization models developed by the Johns Hopkins Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CSSE). To learn more, visit the About page.
